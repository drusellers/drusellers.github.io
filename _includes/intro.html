Building Systems

I love building systems, I love learning about how systems are constructed and designed. There is something about stepping into a new organization and looking at how it was designed. What things does this organization deem important and what doesn't it find important.

For the last 15 years I have been building software systems, and the more that I do it, the more that I realize I have a unique view point on how to build these systems. My undergraduate education is in Economics and Business, I ended up teaching myself how to develop software.

Because of my business background, when I look to start building systems I tend to start with the Business Value Model and then workback from there to the system that needs to be built.
